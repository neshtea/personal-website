My colleague at <a href="https://active-group.de" class="text-accent
hover:underline">Active Group</a>, <a href="https://beathagenlocher.com">Beat
Hagenlocher</a> prepared a workshop for BOB Konf 2026. Unfortunately, he got
sick and I got to fill in spontaneously.

In the interactive tutorial, we watched and discussed the evolution of a <a
href="https://www.solidjs.com" class="text-accent hover:underline">SolidJS</a>
frontend app from state springled all over the place to a place where illegal
states are unrepresentable, `reducers` make state transitions explicit and side
effects are properly encapsulated. Using the approach we discuss, our core
domain logic ends up completely independent of any SolidJS specifics. 

The descriptoin of the workshop and a link to the slides can be found at the <a
href="https://bobkonf.de/2026/hagenlocher.html" class="text-accent
hover:underline">BOB Konf 2026 page for our tutorial</a>.
